Sign up for Coinbase

The easiest way to use cryptocurrency wallets, a great place to start buying crypto.

Consider buying Ethereum instead of Bitcoin-- it's programmable. I believe it's a much better long-hold investment than BTC.

Stay Safe! Use 2FA!

Once you join, go to Settings > Security

Adding your phone number for two-factor authentication is the easiest way. Coinbase will ask you to confirm every action on your smartphone.

Google Authenticator app is recommended for 2FA.

Set up wallet / vault

Crypto wallets are what hold cryptocurrency. Create one for the coin you plan to buy.

Coinbase Accounts

Attach a bank account

Once you add a debit or credit account, head to Buy/Sell.

Any money you send will get stored in a Coinbase wallet. You can move it somewhere else after.

Choose currency

PLEASE only invest money you can afford to lose! Crypto markets are very exciting, uncertain things.

You can increase your weekly max by providing extra photo IDs to Coinbase.
