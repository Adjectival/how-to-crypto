Buy Crypto #2

Stay Safe! Use 2FA!

Once you join, go to Settings > Security

Adding your phone number for two-factor authentication is the easiest way. Coinbase will ask you to confirm every action on your smartphone.

Google Authenticator app is recommended for 2FA.

Set up wallet / vault

Coinbase Accounts

Crypto wallets are what hold cryptocurrency. Create one for the coin you plan to buy.
